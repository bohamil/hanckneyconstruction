What areas do you serve?+

Hackney Construction serves South Central Iowa and the surrounding areas, providing top-notch roofing services for both residential and commercial properties.

How long does a roofing project typically take?+

The duration of a roofing project can vary depending on the size and complexity of the job, but we strive to complete most residential roofing projects within a few days.

Do you offer warranties on your roofing services?+

Yes, we provide comprehensive warranties on both materials and workmanship to ensure your peace of mind and the longevity of your roof.

Can you assist with roofing insurance claims?+

Absolutely. We have extensive experience navigating the insurance claim process and can assist you in securing the coverage you are entitled to for roof repairs or replacements.

What should I do to prepare for a roof installation or repair?+

Preparation typically involves securing any loose items around your property and ensuring clear access to the work area. We will provide specific guidance based on your unique situation before the project begins.
