02 - Fresh Roof

Buy time when your shingles still have life left.

Fresh Roof is a spray-applied treatment that restores flexibility, seals micro-cracks, and improves granule adhesion. For asphalt roofs with solid structure, it can be the fastest way to buy another decade without jumping straight to replacement.

  • Save up to 70% vs. replacement
  • Seals against wind-driven rain
  • Extends roof life 10-15 years
  • Reduces landfill waste
