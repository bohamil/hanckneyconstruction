🚩 5 Questions to Ask Before Hiring a Traveling Roofer

← Back to Blog

After big storms, out-of-town roofers — aka “storm chasers” — swarm small towns with fast offers and flashy flyers. Some are fine. Some are flat-out frauds. Protect your home and your money by asking these:

  1. 📍 Are you licensed to work in this state?
  2. 🏠 Do you have local references?
  3. 🛠 Who installs the roof — your crew or subcontractors?
  4. 📄 Will you give me a written warranty and contract?
  5. 🕵️‍♂️ What happens if I have an issue 6 months later?
