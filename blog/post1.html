💨 What to Do After a Windstorm Hits Your Roof

← Back to Blog

Windstorms can do more damage than you might think — especially to older shingles. Here’s how to assess your home safely and act quickly:

  • 🔍 Check the attic for leaks or wet insulation
  • 🌳 Look for shingles in your yard or gutter downspouts
  • 📸 Take photos for insurance claims
  • 📞 Contact Hackney Construction for a free inspection

Even minor wind damage can lead to costly repairs later. Acting fast protects your home and your wallet.
