🌨️ How Hail Damages Shingles (and How to Spot It)

← Back to Blog

Hail damage doesn’t always look dramatic, but the impacts can shorten the life of your roof. Here’s what to know:

  • 🟤 Loss of granules can expose the shingle base
  • 🔘 Bruising from hail can create weak spots
  • 🧰 Insurance often covers this — but only if it's documented quickly
