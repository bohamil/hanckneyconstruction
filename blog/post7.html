Why Roof Rejuvenation Matters Before Winter

  • Ice Dams: In Indianola and Norwalk, melting and refreezing snow can push water under shingles.
  • Granule Loss: Snow and ice strip away protective granules, leaving shingles vulnerable.
  • Moisture Penetration: Once weakened, melting snow leads to leaks and structural damage.

What is Fresh Roof Rejuvenation?
